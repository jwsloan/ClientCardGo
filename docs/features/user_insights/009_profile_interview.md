# 009 – Natural Profile Interview Experience

## User Story
As a busy professional completing my profile, I want to have a natural conversation about my experience and skills, so that I can share my story in a way that feels authentic and effortless.

## Acceptance Criteria

### Natural Conversation Flow
- The interview feels like a genuine conversation with a knowledgeable colleague
- Questions are thoughtfully sequenced and build on my previous answers
- I can take my time to think and respond
- I can easily edit or expand on my previous responses

### Clear Guidance
- I understand what kind of information would be most valuable to share
- I receive gentle prompts if my answers could use more detail
- I can see my progress through the interview
- I know what to expect next

### Professional Experience
- The conversation helps me articulate my expertise clearly
- I feel confident my skills and experience are being captured accurately
- The tone is professional but friendly
- I can express my career goals and preferences naturally

### Accessibility and Usability
- I can complete the interview using only my keyboard
- Screen readers announce all content clearly
- I can pause and resume the interview when needed
- Error messages are clear and help me fix any issues

## User Scenarios

### Initial Interview
Maria starts her profile interview. The conversation begins with open-ended questions about her career journey, making her feel comfortable sharing her story. Follow-up questions help her highlight key achievements she might have otherwise forgotten to mention.

### Detailed Exploration
John appreciates how the interview digs deeper into areas where he has the most experience. When he mentions leading a team, the conversation naturally explores his leadership style and achievements.

### Thoughtful Breaks
Sarah needs to step away during her interview. She easily saves her progress and returns later, picking up the conversation naturally where she left off.
